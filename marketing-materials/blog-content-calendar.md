## POST STRUCTURE TEMPLATE

Each post follows this structure:
1. **SEO-optimized title** (60 chars max)
2. **Compelling meta description** (155 chars max)
3. **Introduction with hook** (150-200 words)
4. **Table of contents** (for longer posts)
5. **Main content** (1500-2500 words)
6. **Visual callout boxes** (tips, warnings, stats)
7. **Conclusion with key takeaways**
8. **CTA to relevant service/audit**
