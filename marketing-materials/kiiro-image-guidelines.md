## IMAGE COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### 1. Hero/Banner Images

**Dimensions:** 1920x1080 (16:9) or 1200x630 (OG standard)

**Composition:**
- Dark background (60-80% of frame)
- Yellow accent elements (10-20% of frame)
- Subject/content in remaining space
- Leave safe zones for text overlay

**Examples:**
- Dark gradient background with glowing yellow geometric shapes
- Abstract tech visualization in black with yellow data points/nodes
- Minimalist desk setup silhouette with yellow lamp glow
- Website mockup on dark screen with yellow cursor/highlight

### 2. Blog Post Images

**Dimensions:** 1200x630 (featured) or 800x450 (inline)

**Style:**
- Consistent dark frames with yellow accent lines
- Icon-style illustrations for concepts
- Abstract representations of topics
- Minimal text, let visuals tell story

**Topic-Specific Guides:**

| Topic | Visual Approach |
|-------|-----------------|
| SEO | Magnifying glass on dark grid, yellow highlights on keywords |
| Performance | Speed lines, clock/gauge with yellow needle |
| CRO | Arrow pointing up, growth chart with yellow line |
| Design | Wireframe/UI elements in yellow outline on dark |
| Business | Handshake, contract, growth icons in yellow |
| Mobile | Phone silhouette with yellow screen elements |

### 3. Social Media Images

**Instagram Post:** 1080x1080
- Centered composition
- Bold typography on dark
- Yellow accent shapes

**Twitter/X Card:** 1200x675
- Horizontal layout
- Left: Visual, Right: Text area

**LinkedIn:** 1200x627
- Professional, clean
- Less aggressive yellow than social

### 4. Icon & Illustration Style

**Characteristics:**
- Line weight: 2-3px stroke
- Rounded corners (2-4px radius)
- Flat design with subtle gradients
- Yellow fills for emphasis
- Dark outlines

**Do:**
- Use kiiro yellow for active/selected states
- Keep shapes simple and recognizable
- Maintain consistent line weights

**Don't:**
- Use full-color illustrations
- Over-complicate simple concepts
- Mix illustration styles

## THINGS TO AVOID

### Don't Use:
- ❌ Pure white backgrounds (unless text-heavy graphics)
- ❌ Multiple competing accent colors
- ❌ Cluttered compositions
- ❌ Low contrast elements
- ❌ Outdated design trends
- ❌ Stock photo aesthetics
- ❌ Overly complex illustrations
- ❌ Yellow-dominant images (yellow should accent, not dominate)

### Always Ensure:
- ✅ Dark backgrounds (60%+ of image)
- ✅ Strategic yellow use (highlights, accents)
- ✅ Clear focal point
- ✅ Professional, not playful
- ✅ Consistent with brand feel
